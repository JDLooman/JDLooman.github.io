Ninjas on Trampolines:

A two-to-four player arcade fighting style game in which players slice their opponents to splatter paint on the battlefield. Use trampolines to do tricks and fight for aerial superiority. Having the most paint on the battlefield will make you victorious, but be careful because your opponents can paint over what you have already.

Team Size: 7
Role: Producer and UI/UX
Tools: Miro, Trello, Unity and Game Design Document

Helpless:

You are a machine with a single purpose; survive. Helpless is a single-player experimental simulation game designed to allow players to experience the existential dread of being a machine tasked to clean up its mess for an eternity.

Team Size: 5
Role: Producer and UI/UX
Tools: Trello, Unity and Game Design Document

Exploding Apples:

Team Size: 1
Role: Developer
Tools: Trello and Unity

2023

Icarus VR:

Icarus VR is a virtual reality game that has fast-paced gun shooting and skill-based bow and arrow mechanics. Players compete in sky islands, each with its own unique features and challenges. The game offers a variety of game modes, such as Target Frenzy, Quick Draw, and Archery, each having a server-side leaderboard.

Team Size: 4
Role: Producer, Level Design and UI/UX
Tools: Miro, Trello and Unity

Changeling:

ChangelingVR is a virtual reality first-person interactive mystery founded upon the ideas of magical realism and a sense of unease and wonder. You play as detective Aurelia Walker, a private eye gifted with the unique ability to dream-walk, allowing her to see the thoughts and memories of anyone she touches. This gift leads the Summers-Manalo family to hire you, their last resort in discovering what is wrong with their baby. As you make contact with the mother, the father, and their two kids, you must use your mystical powers, as well as your skills of deduction, to enter their minds and make your way through their manifested hopes and fears to save the young child.

Team Size: 6
Role: Lead Level Designer
Tools: Clickup, Unreal 4.2 and Level Design Document

Shaping the Lives of the Future

Hi there,

My name is Justin Looman and am currently a 4th year at Rochester Institute of Technology. I am a game developer who loves to create and balance worlds for myself and others to enjoy and explore. I have experience leading multiple projects like Ninjas on Trampolines, ChangelingVR, Icarus, and Helpless. Through my time leading these projects I have learned how to bring together multiple disciplines and create the best possible product. My most recent project Ninjas on Trampolines is currently going through the Maker program at RIT with MAGIC Spell Studios.

My passion for gaming goes all the way back to when I was a kid. Playing games and getting lost for hours, often getting me into trouble, made many core memories. Now I'm looking to return the favor and bring many good memories to those experienced and new to gaming.
